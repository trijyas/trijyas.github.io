GridFest 2026

GridFest 2026 is a celebration of 5 years of Grid App for Artists - a journey shaped by creativity, learning, and an incredible artist community. What started as a simple idea to help artists draw with confidence has grown into a platform used by creators across different styles and skill levels. GridFest is our way of celebrating this milestone and giving back to the artists who made it possible.
Over the past five years, we've seen artists grow, experiment, and push their limits using our tools. The support, feedback, and love from the community is what kept us going.

GridFest 2026 features a mega giveaway event where artists are invited to :
Share their art journey
Show how they use Grid App (or its features) creatively
Compete for exciting art kit prizes
